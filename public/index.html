Consciousness and Human Awareness

What is Consciousness?

Consciousness is the state of being aware of and responsive to one's surroundings. It is the ability to experience thoughts, feelings, and sensations.

What is Human Awareness?

Human awareness is the state of being aware of oneself and one's surroundings. It is the ability to think about one's own thoughts and experiences, and to understand how one's behavior affects others.
